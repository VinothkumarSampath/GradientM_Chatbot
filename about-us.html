Our Story

GradientM IT Consulting & Services, founded in July 2018 and headquartered in Bangalore, the Silicon Valley of India, was born from a vision of empowering businesses to thrive in the dynamic AI revolution.

Our CEO & Founder Ravindra Talwai brings in rock solid experience and insights into Global IT for past 25+ years. In his leadership roles in Cognizant and Wipro Technologies, he engaged and enabled CXOs of Fortune 500 companies in IT Transformation programs.

We at Gradient M have built capabilities to solve your intricate IT problems by our service offerings in IT Strategy Consulting, Cloud, Risk and Compliance and Data & AI.

We engage high impact talent to accelerate your Digital transformation and scale up your business to greater success.

In the past 6 years we have engaged and built strategic partnerships with SaaS companies, Global IT providers, India capability centres and mid-size businesses.
